## Who is Owli-AI for?

Owli-AI is designed for:

- People with visual impairment or blindness  
- People with hearing impairment  
- People with combined hearing and visual impairment  
- Anyone who wants to use digital assistance for better orientation and comprehension  

The applications are modular and can be used individually or in combination.

## The Owli-AI applications

Each app fulfills a clearly defined task. Together they form a continuous assistance system.

### Way-Buddy
Digital orientation aid for detecting walkable paths with acoustic feedback.

### Assist
AI-supported scene analysis with follow-up questions, voice control, and context-based assistance.

### Magnify
Mobile magnifier with contrast filters, OCR, and magnifying mirror.

### Hear-Clean
Real-time transcription and optimized speech output for better speech intelligibility.

### Zoom
Desktop magnification with integrated reading features, currently for Windows, Linux in planning.

## Our principles

- Focus on real daily-life situations  
- Processing directly on-device whenever possible  
- Clear and understandable assistance instead of technical complexity  
- Transparency in privacy and development  

## Further development

Owli-AI is continuously developed. The goal is to connect mobile and desktop assistance even more closely and combine intelligent functions in a meaningful way.
